Why this archive exists

Archaeological information is often scattered in reports, PDFs, and personal notes. This project gathers site records into a lightweight, searchable collection to improve preservation and discovery.

  • Preservation of site data over time
  • Searchable, mobile-friendly access
  • Community contributions welcomed
